About Me:

How does a Manager become a writer and then a writer games programmer? If you have a need to create, something feels deeply wrong when you don't excerise your creativity daily. Next, what started off as a goal of ensuring my scripts made it into game jam games ended with me switching career paths.

  • Gameplay Programmer for several indie projects.
  • Manager at three Fortune 500 Companies.
